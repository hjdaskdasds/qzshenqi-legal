About the service

Research that helps builders reject weak ideas earlier.

Opportunity Intel is a founder-operated research subscription that organizes public pain signals into practical product decisions.

Why Opportunity Intel exists

Independent builders rarely lack ideas. The harder problem is deciding which problem deserves another week of attention. Community research can help, but manually reading threads and review feeds is noisy, repetitive, and easy to overinterpret.

Opportunity Intel turns that work into concise briefs. Each opportunity starts with a public source and ends with a narrow buyer, MVP hypothesis, pricing test, distribution angle, and explicit risk.

What the service is

  • A recurring research feed built from publicly accessible user discussions and reviews.
  • A comparison system for pain clarity, buyer clarity, urgency, build scope, and competition risk.
  • A starting point for interviews, landing-page tests, and paid manual pilots.

What the service is not

  • It is not a promise that an idea will produce revenue or become a successful business.
  • It is not legal, financial, investment, or market-size advice.
  • It is not affiliated with Reddit, Apple, or products referenced in the research.
